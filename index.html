FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why do you ask personal info on signup?

Answer

Our priority is that you can find people who really interest you, that is why we want you to complete your profile correctly and honestly, so that the people with whom you have a match, are the right people for you. Likewise, we use these data only for matching purposes, they are not shared with third parties.

How do you handle my personal info?

Answer

We only use data such as your name, email, date of birth, and profile picture, we only use this information when you are in the application, trying to target the advertising you see within Nearby Connect.

How can i delete my account data?

Answer

We regret that you do not want to continue using Nearby Connect. For now, to delete your account, you just have to access your profile by clicking your profile picture on the top menu bar, and then scroll to the bottom of your profile page, you will find the link to delete your account and its associated collected stored data.

How can i control app notifications?

Answer

From your profile you can decide what type of notifications you want to receive, such as notifications of visits, post likes, chat rooms, private chats or other functions by simply turning them off.

Can i report a user?

Answer

Yes you can, Reporting users is a fundamental resource within the application, this functionality allow us to maintain a pleasant and safe environment within the application, if you want to report someone, you have three options, on the one hand, it may be due to inappropriate content, on the other, inappropriate communication, or also for identifying a false profile, Spam or Scam. Finally, we will review your report in detail, and proceed with the relevant action.
