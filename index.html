PRIVACY & CONTENT POLICY

This privacy policy sets out how Nearby Connect uses and protects the information that you provide upon registration to use Nearby Connect chat and social service. Nearby Connect is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should incase you are required to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using Nearby Connect application, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

• We do not tolerate any content of a pornographic, violent nature or that promotes a bad environment. We have a user-generated content moderation system that control this type of inappropriate content.

THE INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT WHEN YOU USE NEARBY CONNECT

We collect only the following information from your mobile device that install Nearby Connect application:

• Information about your location if you give us permission to do so ("Location Data") We may store and use this data to provide features of our service, to improve and personalize our service.
• We allow outside companies to post advertisements and collect certain anonymous information when you onboard and use our application.
• These companies may collect anonymous information, such as your Google advertising ID, your device type and version, browsing activity, location and other technical data related to your device, to serve advertisements.


THE INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT WHEN YOU USE NEARBY CONNECT

We do no collect the following information from your mobile device that install Nearby Connect application:

• We do not collect informations such as names, addresses or other personal and private information from Nearby Connect users.
• When you use the chat and social service on Nearby Connect mobile application we may or may not record information that is posted in public chat spaces.
• The information that you provide in a private conversation with a third party will NEVER be collected, moderated, nor viewed by anyone, other than you and the third party with whom you exchange information. Therefore Nearby Connect and its administrators will not be responsible for your private activities with third parties.
